What Is a Private AI Setup?

A private AI setup gives your business access to AI tools without putting sensitive company information into public systems. It can help with documents, internal questions, workflows, training material, customer support drafts, and business organization.

What We Set Up

Private AI Assistant

A secure AI assistant for your business that can help answer questions, organize information, and support daily tasks.

Business Document Support

We help prepare your business documents so your AI system can search, summarize, and use them more effectively.

Local or Controlled Hosting

We help choose a setup that fits your privacy needs, whether that is local hardware, a secure computer, or a controlled private system.

Who This Is For

This service is designed for local businesses that want the benefits of AI while keeping control of their data. It is a good fit for offices, service businesses, contractors, medical-adjacent businesses, professional services, and teams with private internal information.

How the Process Works

1. Discovery

We learn what your business needs, what information you want to protect, and what tasks AI could help with.

2. Setup

We build and configure the private AI system, organize the needed files, and prepare it for your business use.

3. Training & Support

We show your team how to use it safely and help improve the setup as your business grows.
